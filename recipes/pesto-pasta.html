Pesto-Pasta

Description

Recipe for spaghetti with chicken and green pesto, a lovely everyday meal!

Home made pesto and roasted pine nuts makes it extra tasty.

If you want a healthier alternative, use whole grain pasta.

Ingredients

  • 600 g chicken
  • 400 g spaghetti

Pesto

  • 1 pot of fresh basil
  • 50 g pine nuts
  • 25 g parmesan
  • 1,5 dl olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • lemon juice

Steps

  1. Chicken: Preheat oven to 200 degrees. Fry the chicken, filet or breast, with oil in a pan until it gets a nice golden color.
  2. Remove the chicken from the pan and put it in the middle of the oven for about 25 minutes.
  3. Boil the pasta using the directions from the package.
  4. Pesto: Mix basil, pine nuts, parmesan, olive oil, salt and pepper in a bowl using an immersion blender.
  5. Mix the pesto with the pasta and cut the chicken in slices. Put the Chicken over the pasta, and top it with a sprinkle of pine nuts.
